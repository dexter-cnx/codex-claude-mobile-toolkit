# Task Pack: Refactor Safely

Use this pack for structural cleanup.

## Rules
- Separate structural cleanup from behavior changes.
- Preserve external behavior unless explicitly changing it.
- Explain why the refactor is worth the churn.
- Reduce complexity, not just move it around.
- Keep migration risk visible.

## Deliverable shape
- problem in current structure
- target structure
- migration steps
- risk notes
- test impact
